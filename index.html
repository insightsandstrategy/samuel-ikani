About Samuel Ikani

Samuel Ikani is an accomplished Development Science Specialist, evaluation expert, and RCT methodologist with a strong track record in international development, research, and policy advisory. He works across governments, NGOs, and global development organisations, designing monitoring and evaluation systems, conducting impact assessments, and providing strategic advisory to strengthen institutions and improve outcomes. His approach combines structured thinking, analytical rigor, academic framework and hands-on application then, implementation to ensure that programmes deliver measurable, evidence-driven results.
